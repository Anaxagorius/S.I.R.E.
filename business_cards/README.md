## Generating the PDF

1. Open `SIRE_Business_Cards.html` in **Chrome** or **Edge**
2. Press **Ctrl+P** (Windows/Linux) or **⌘P** (Mac)
3. Set:
   - **Destination:** Save as PDF
   - **Paper size:** Letter (8.5 × 11 in)
   - **Margins:** None
   - **Background graphics:** ✅ Enabled (required for colours)
4. Click **Save**

## Printing Tips

- Cards are sized to the standard **3.5 × 2 inch** business card format
- Print on **heavy card stock** (250–350 gsm) for the best feel
- Use a guillotine or scored perforated sheets for clean cuts
- For double-sided printing: print Sheet 1 (fronts), flip paper, print Sheet 2 (backs)
